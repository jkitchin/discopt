* Problem
:PROPERTIES:
:SOURCE: Hillier & Lieberman, "Introduction to Operations Research", 10th ed., Chapter 3
:CLASS: LP
:KNOWN_OPTIMAL: 21.0
:N_VARIABLES: 2
:N_CONSTRAINTS: 2
:END:

\begin{align}
\max_{x_1, x_2} \quad & 5 x_1 + 4 x_2 \\
\text{s.t.} \quad & 6 x_1 + 4 x_2 \leq 24 \\
& x_1 + 2 x_2 \leq 6 \\
& 0 \leq x_1 \leq 4, \quad 0 \leq x_2 \leq 6
\end{align}

The optimal is at the intersection of the two constraints: solving
$6x_1 + 4x_2 = 24$ and $x_1 + 2x_2 = 6$ gives $x_1 = 3, x_2 = 1.5$
with objective $5(3) + 4(1.5) = 21$.
